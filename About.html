About School

Odisha Adarsha Vidyalaya Sangathan was established on September 19th, 2015, as a society under The Society Registration Act of Odisha. Odisha Adarsha Vidyalaya was founded by the Department of School and Mass Education, Govt. of Odisha The General Body is the apex body of the Sangathan headed by Hon’ble Minister, School & Mass Education as Chairman. The Executive Body is headed by DC-cum-ACS as Chairman.

Progress

100 Odisha Adarsha Vidyalayas(OAVs) have already started functioning from academic session 2016–17 and 60 more academic session 2017-18.New 30 Schools Opened In 2018-19 And 24 More In 2019-20. As Of Now a total of 214 OAV running in Odisha. Amongst the 100 first phase school Class-XI Science Opened in 30 OAVs and expected to open in rest schools next school. Dr Bijaya Kumar Sahoo has been appointed as Advisor with rank and status of Minister of State by the Odisha Government. He briefed CM the road map for 314 high quality schools
